Articles

  • Why pinch beats grab
    A clenched fist tires fast and gives one bit of information; pinch measures a continuous distance between the two most stable landmarks on a hand. That's why every modern mid-air UI uses it.
  • The One Euro filter, briefly
    A simple low-pass filter whose cutoff frequency increases with measured speed. Cuts jitter on slow movements without adding lag to fast ones.
  • Topology constraints
    Per-axis smoothing breaks the kinematic chain when one fingertip glitches. A bone-length EMA puts joints back where their parents and segment lengths say they should be.
